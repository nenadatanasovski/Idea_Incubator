# AI-Powered Recipe App - Feature List

## 1. Core Recipe Features

### Recipe Discovery

- **Smart Search** - Natural language search ("quick dinner with chicken and no dairy")
- **Browse by Category** - Cuisine, meal type, dietary restrictions, cooking time
- **Trending Recipes** - Popular recipes based on seasonality and user activity
- **Recipe of the Day** - Curated daily recommendation
- **Random Recipe Generator** - "Surprise me" button for inspiration

### Recipe Display

- **Step-by-Step Instructions** - Clear, numbered cooking steps
- **Ingredient List** - With quantities and unit conversions
- **Nutrition Information** - Calories, macros, vitamins, allergens
- **Cooking Time Estimates** - Prep, cook, and total time
- **Difficulty Rating** - Beginner, intermediate, advanced
- **Serving Size Adjuster** - Auto-scale ingredients for different portions
- **Print-Friendly View** - Optimized layout for printing

---

## 2. AI-Powered Features

### Personalization Engine

- **Taste Profile Learning** - Learns preferences from ratings and cooking history
- **Smart Recommendations** - "You might like" based on past behavior
- **Dietary Preference Adaptation** - Auto-filters based on restrictions
- **Skill-Level Matching** - Suggests recipes appropriate to cooking ability

### AI Recipe Generation

- **Create from Ingredients** - "What can I make with..." feature
- **Recipe Remix** - AI variations on existing recipes
- **Fusion Creator** - Combine cuisines or cooking styles
- **Healthier Alternatives** - AI-suggested substitutions for health goals
- **Budget-Friendly Versions** - Cheaper ingredient alternatives

### Intelligent Assistance

- **AI Cooking Coach** - Real-time chat for cooking questions
- **Technique Explainer** - "What does 'fold' mean?" explanations
- **Troubleshooting Help** - "My sauce is too thin" solutions
- **Ingredient Substitutions** - Smart alternatives for missing items
- **Wine/Beverage Pairing** - AI-suggested drink pairings

### Visual AI

- **Photo-to-Recipe** - Upload food photo, get similar recipes
- **Ingredient Scanner** - Camera identifies ingredients
- **Plating Suggestions** - AI presentation tips
- **Doneness Detection** - Camera checks if food looks ready
