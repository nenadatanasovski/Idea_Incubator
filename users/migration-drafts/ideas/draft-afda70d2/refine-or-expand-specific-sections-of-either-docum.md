## Problem Statement

**The Idea-to-Execution Chasm**

Most people have ideas they believe in but lack the technical skills, time, or network to bring them to life. The traditional path—learning to code, hiring developers, or finding a co-founder—takes months or years, by which time the window has closed or motivation has faded.

- **72% of aspiring entrepreneurs** cite "technical skills" as their biggest barrier
- **Average time** from idea to MVP: 6-12 months
- **Most ideas die** not from being bad, but from never being tested

---

## Solution Overview

**Vibe is an autonomous AI platform with four integrated capabilities:**

1. **Idea → Validated Concept**: AI agents research your market, identify competitors, and stress-test assumptions before you build anything

2. **Concept → Built App**: Self-improving agents (SIA loops) translate validated concepts into functional applications—no coding required

3. **App → Connected Users**: Proactive networking agents identify and reach potential early adopters, partners, and investors

4. **Continuous Optimization**: The system learns from each cycle, improving validation accuracy and build quality over time
