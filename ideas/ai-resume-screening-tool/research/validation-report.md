- EEOC removed guidance documents but federal anti-discrimination law still applies
- **Mobley v. Workday (2025)**: Landmark class action certified, potentially affecting millions

### Implications for This Idea

**Opportunity:** Compliance-focused AI screening becomes a differentiator
**Risk:** Regulatory fragmentation makes multi-state deployment complex
**Strategy:** Build compliance features as core, not afterthought

**Verdict: ⚠️ Regulatory headwinds are real, but create differentiation opportunity**

   - Pricing: Premium for compliance ($200-500/month)

2. **"The SMB Champion"**
   - Target: Companies hiring 20-100 people/year

   - Pricing: $49-149/month

   - Differentiator: Deep integrations, no data silos
   - Pricing: $100-300/month
