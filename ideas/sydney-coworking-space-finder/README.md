# Sydney Coworking Space Finder

## Problem
Remote workers and freelancers in Sydney struggle to find available coworking spaces, especially on short notice. Existing platforms are fragmented and don't show real-time availability.

## Solution
A mobile-first platform that aggregates coworking spaces across Greater Sydney with real-time availability, instant booking, and location-based search.

## Target Market
- Remote workers in Sydney (estimated 500,000+)
- Freelancers and consultants
- Small business owners needing meeting rooms
- Corporate teams needing flexible workspace

## Market Size
- Sydney coworking market: $450M AUD
- Australian coworking market: $2.1B AUD
- Global coworking market: $15B USD

## Competition
- WeWork (global player)
- Hub Australia
- Tank Stream Labs
- Fishburners
- Google Maps (partial solution)
