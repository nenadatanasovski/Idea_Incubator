# Target User Personas: AI Fridge-to-Meal Recipe App

## Document Overview

This artifact presents detailed user personas for the AI-powered fridge scanning recipe app. These personas are designed to guide product decisions, marketing strategy, and feature prioritization.

- Eat healthier without extensive meal planning

## Summary: Key Insights

### Universal Needs (All Personas)

1. **Speed**: Scanning must be under 30 seconds
2. **Accuracy**: Ingredient recognition must be >90% accurate
3. **Relevance**: Recipes must match actual available ingredients
4. **Simplicity**: Core flow must be intuitive from day 1

### Differentiation Opportunities

1. **Nutrition integration** (Marcus's segment) = premium pricing power
