# Idea Types

> **AUTHORITATIVE**: This file defines all valid idea types/domains.

## Type Definitions

| Type          | Code        | Description                                     | Examples                              |
| ------------- | ----------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Business**  | `business`  | Revenue-generating ventures, startups, products | SaaS products, marketplaces, services |
| **Creative**  | `creative`  | Art, content, design projects                   | Books, films, music, games, artwork   |
| **Technical** | `technical` | Tools, systems, engineering projects            | Libraries, frameworks, infrastructure |
| **Personal**  | `personal`  | Self-improvement, lifestyle, hobbies            | Habits, skills, relationships         |
| **Research**  | `research`  | Knowledge exploration, experiments              | Studies, papers, investigations       |

## Type Selection Guidelines

### Business

Choose when the idea:

- Has a clear revenue model
- Targets customers who would pay
- Involves building a company or product for sale
- Focuses on market opportunity

### Creative

Choose when the idea:

- Is primarily artistic or expressive
- Focuses on creating content or experiences
- Success measured by audience engagement, not revenue
- Personal creative fulfillment is primary goal

### Technical

Choose when the idea:

- Solves a technical problem
- Is primarily a tool or utility
- Target users are developers or technical people
- Could be open source

### Personal

Choose when the idea:

- Focuses on self-improvement
- Affects only yourself or close circle
- Not intended to be shared publicly
- Relates to habits, health, relationships

### Research

Choose when the idea:

- Is exploratory without defined outcome
- Involves learning or investigation
- Seeks to answer a question
- Generates knowledge rather than products

## Hybrid Ideas

Many ideas span multiple types. Choose the primary type based on:

1. Primary motivation (money vs art vs learning)
2. Target audience (customers vs users vs self)
3. Success metric (revenue vs engagement vs knowledge)

Document secondary types in tags:

```yaml
type: technical
tags:
  - has-business-potential
  - creative-elements
```
